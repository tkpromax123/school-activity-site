Exercise Types for 30 Minutes a Day

Pick a type, follow the 30-minute plan, and split it into mini sessions if you like.

How to Use These Plans

Target: 30 minutes of moderate activity daily (e.g., 3 × 10 minutes). Choose any plan below; swap exercises you don’t like for ones you enjoy.

  • Warm-up (3–5 min): light jog/walk, arm circles, hip rolls.
  • Main set (20–24 min): the core of the plan.
  • Cool-down (3–5 min): easy walk + stretches.

Cardio (Moderate) — 30-Minute Plan

View routine
  • Warm-up: 5 min brisk walk.
  • Main: 20 min continuous movement — choose one or mix:
    • Brisk walk / light jog around the oval.
    • Cycle (stationary or outdoor).
    • Skipping rope (intervals: 1 min on / 30s off).
    • Shadow boxing or dance flow.
  • Cool-down: 5 min easy walk + calf/quad stretches.

Bodyweight Strength — 30-Minute Plan (No Equipment)

View routine
  • Warm-up: 4 min (high knees, arm swings, hip hinges).
  • Main (4 rounds): 40s work / 20s rest
    • Squats → Push-ups (wall/knee/standard) → Reverse lunges → Plank
    • Rest 60s between rounds
  • Cool-down: 4–5 min stretch (hamstrings, chest, hips).

Flexibility & Mobility — 30-Minute Flow

View routine
  • 5 min gentle walk + shoulder rolls
  • 20 min sequence:
    • World’s Greatest Stretch (each side)
    • Cat–Cow → Thoracic rotations
    • Hip flexor stretch → Glute stretch
    • Hamstring sweep → Calf stretch
  • 5 min breathing + easy walk

Mixed Circuit — 30-Minute Plan

View routine
  • Warm-up: 5 min brisk walk.
  • AMRAP (20 min): Repeat as many rounds as possible at a steady pace:
    • 10 Squats
    • 8 Push-ups (any level)
    • 12 Walking lunges (6/leg)
    • 20 Jumping jacks
    • 30s Plank
  • Cool-down: 5 min easy walk + stretch.

At-School Mini Sessions (3 × 10 Minutes)

  • Before school (10): 5 min brisk walk + 5 min mobility.
  • Recess (10): 5 × (45s fast walk / 45s easy) around a safe loop.
  • Lunch (10): Quick circuit — 2 rounds: 10 squats, 10 push-ups, 20 jumping jacks, 20s plank.
